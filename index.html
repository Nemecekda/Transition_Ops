TRANSITION OPS

MISSION-READY TOOLS FOR MILITARY TRANSITION

Free mobile-friendly transition toolkit built for service members, veterans, and Guard/Reserve personnel. Consolidates the scattered resources you need to navigate ETS, separation, retirement, and post-service life into one operationally-focused platform. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads.

CORE CAPABILITIES

  • VA Combined Rating Calculator (VA Math) — Calculate your combined disability rating using the official VA formula
  • VA Disability Pay Estimator — Project monthly compensation based on rating and dependents
  • SkillBridge Employer Feed — Current opportunities for your military-to-civilian transition
  • State Tax Intelligence on Military Retired Pay — All 50 states + DC, color-coded with MSRRA guidance
  • Final PCS Entitlements — JTR weight allowances, DLA timing, MALT calculator, move timeline
  • Smart Reminder Engine — Personalized timeline alerts for BDD filing, SBP election, TAMP, TSP rollover, and every other deadline that costs money if missed
  • Guard/Reserve Dedicated Dashboard — Separate flows for drilling Reservists and gray-area retirees
  • DD214 Verification Guide — Line-by-line checklist so you don't leave errors on your separation document
  • Career Pathway Mapper — Translate your MOS/AFSC to civilian roles with realistic bridge assessments
  • Transition Readiness Score — Self-assessment that identifies gaps in your transition plan

BUILT BY A VETERAN

Founded by Dean Nemecek — Army veteran, Senior HR professional (SPHR, SHRM-SCP), and founder of Veteran Bridge Solutions. Developed on personal time to fill the gap that leaves too many service members flat-footed at separation. Currently in distribution to Wisconsin National Guard transition staff, local MFRCs, and CVSO networks.
